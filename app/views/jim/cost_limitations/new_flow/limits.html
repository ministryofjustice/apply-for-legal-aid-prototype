Non-molestation order

Scope limits

Emergency certificate

Form of service: Full representation

Work you can do: Limited to all steps necessary to apply for an interim order; where application is made without notice to include representation on the return date.

Substantive certificate

Form of service: Full representation

Work you can do: As to proceedings under Part IV Family Law Act 1996 limited to all steps up to and including obtaining and serving a final order and in the event of breach leading to the exercise of a power of arrest to representation on the consideration of the breach by the court (but excluding applying for a warrant of arrest, if not attached, and representation in contempt proceedings).
