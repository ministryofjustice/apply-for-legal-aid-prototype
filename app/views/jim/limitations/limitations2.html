Cost and scope limits

There are limits to the costs you can incur and work you can do based on the proceeding you selected.

Proceeding

Non-molestation order (domestic abuse)

 The costs you can incur

£5,000

 The work you can do

You can:

  • carry out all steps up to and including serving a final order
  • represent someone arrested for breaching a non-molestation order on the consideration of the court

You cannot:

  • apply for an arrest warrant
  • represent someone in contempt proceedings
