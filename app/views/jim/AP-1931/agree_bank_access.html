Do you agree to share 3 months of bank statements with the LAA via TrueLayer?

We only use your bank statements to support your legal aid application. We do not use them for anything else.
What will TrueLayer be able to see?

TrueLayer will have one-off, read-only access to your:

  • account details (for example, your account number and sort code)
  • bank statements and transaction details
  • account balances
  • Direct Debits and standing orders

They will not:

  • store your bank account details
  • see your online banking sign-in details
  • share your details with anyone other than the LAA
  • have any ongoing access to your bank account details
