What your client has to do

Your client needs to:

  • access 'Apply for legal aid' using the link we'll send them in an email
  • agree to share their bank account details with the LAA via TrueLayer

They'll then be redirected to their bank's website so they can:

  • sign in to their online banking
  • agree to give TrueLayer one-time access to their account details

Once they've done this, your client will be returned to 'Apply for legal aid'.

They'll then need to answer questions about their income and outgoings.

If your client does not agree to the above, you'll need to complete the application in CCMS.
