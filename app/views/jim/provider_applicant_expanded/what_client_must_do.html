What your client has to do

Your client needs to access 'Apply for legal aid' using a link we'll send them in an email.

They'll then be redirected to their bank’s website or app where they can:

  • sign in to their online banking
  • give TrueLayer permission to collect 3 months of bank statements on behalf of the LAA

Once they've done this, your client will be returned to 'Apply for legal aid' so they can answer questions about their income and outgoings.
