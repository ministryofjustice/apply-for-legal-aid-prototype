Do you agree to share your bank account details with the LAA via TrueLayer?

We only use your financial information to support your legal aid application. We do not use it for anything else.
What information will TrueLayer be able to see?

TrueLayer will be able to see your:

  • account details (for example, your full name and address)
  • bank statements and transaction details
  • account balances
  • Direct Debits and standing orders

TrueLayer will not:

  • have access to your online banking username and password
  • share your bank account details with anyone other than the LAA
  • have any ongoing access to your bank account
