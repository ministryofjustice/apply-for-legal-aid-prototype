Cookies on Apply for legal aid

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use Apply for legal aid, such as the pages you visit.

Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the service so we can improve it based on user needs.

We do not allow Google to use or share the data about how you use this site.

Google Analytics sets cookies that store anonymised information about:

  • how you got to the site
  • the pages you visit on Apply for legal aid, and how long you spend on each page
  • what you click on while you're visiting the site

Strictly necessary cookies

These essential cookies do things like remember your progress through a form.

They always need to be on.
