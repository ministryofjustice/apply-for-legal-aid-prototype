Declaration

By submitting this application, you agree that:

  • [client] has instructed [legal aid provider] to represent them
  • you've gone through the application with your client
  • you’ve explained the statutory charge to your client
  • the information you've provided is complete and correct

Client declaration

[client] agrees that:

  • they’ve instructed [legal aid provider] to represent them
  • the Legal Aid Agency (LAA) can share their information with other government departments like the Department for Work and Pensions and HM Revenue and Customs
  • the LAA can check their details with bank and credit reference agencies
  • they may have to pay towards legal aid
  • they may have to repay legal costs if they keep or gain property or money at the end of the case (the 'statutory charge')
  • the information they’ve given is complete and correct
  • they’ll report any changes to their financial situation immediately
!

If your client gives wrong or incomplete information, does not report changes, or is found to have committed benefit fraud, they may:

  • be prosecuted
  • need to pay a financial penalty
  • have their legal aid stopped and have to pay back the costs
