Apply for civil legal aid

Who can apply

Civil or family legal aid providers can apply for civil legal aid in domestic violence or abuse cases.

Use this service if your client is single, or the case is against their partner.

Do not use this service if your client is:

  • self-employed
  • a member of the armed forces

Use the Client and Cost Management System (CCMS) for all other applications.
