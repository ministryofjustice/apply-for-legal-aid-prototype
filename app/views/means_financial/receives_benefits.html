What not to include

Government Cost of Living Payments

Government Cost of Living Payments are disregarded from the financial eligibility calculation, for example:

Pensioner Cost of Living Payment
Cost of Living Payment
Disability Cost of Living Payment
Household support fund
Council tax rebate
Energy Bills Support Scheme (EBSS)
Payments for those who are not served by mains gas
