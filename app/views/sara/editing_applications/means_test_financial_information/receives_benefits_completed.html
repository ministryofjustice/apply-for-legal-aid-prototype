Client's means assessment

Does your client get any benefits, charitable or government payments?

For example, Child Benefit or tax credits.

Do not include Housing Benefit, Government Cost of Living Payments, or any other disregarded benefits and payments.

What not to include

Government Cost of Living Payments

Government Cost of Living Payments are disregarded from the financial eligibility calculation, for example:

  • Pensioner Cost of Living Payment
  • Cost of Living Payment
  • Disability Cost of Living Payment
  • Household support fund
  • Council tax rebate
  • Energy Bills Support Scheme (EBSS)
  • Payments for those who are not served by mains gas

Disregarded benefits and payments

The following are disregarded from the financial eligibility calculation. They are known as mandatory disregards.

Carer and disability

  • Attendance Allowance
  • Carer's Allowance
  • Community Care Direct Payments
  • Constant Attendance Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Exceptionally Severe Disablement Allowance
  • Independent Living Fund
  • Payments on account of benefit
  • Personal Independence Payments (PIP)
  • Severe Disablement Allowance
  • Special Education Needs (SEN) Direct Payment
  • Welsh Independent Living Grant

Low income

  • Budgeting Advance
  • Housing Benefit
  • Social Fund Funeral Payment
  • Social Fund payments
  • Universal Credit advance payments

Other

  • Armed Forces Independence Payment
  • Compensation for miscarriage of justice
  • Foster Carers Allowance
  • Grenfell Tower Resident's Discretionary Fund
  • Grenfell Tower fire victims payments
  • Infected Blood Support Scheme (includes Infected Blood Interim Compensation Payment Scheme, Infected Blood Further Interim Compensation Payment Scheme, Infected Blood Compensation Scheme or earlier support schemes)
  • Scotland and Northern Ireland Redress Schemes for historical child abuse
  • Modern Slavery Victim Care Contract (MSVCC) payments
  • War Pensions Scheme payments
  • War Widow(er) Pension
  • Windrush Compensation Scheme Payments
