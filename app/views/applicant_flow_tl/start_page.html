We need to check if you qualify financially for legal aid.

How we do this

The Legal Aid Agency (LAA) needs one-time access to your bank account information so we can download 3 months of bank statements.

We do this to see the kinds of payments you make and receive. This helps us work out if you can get free legal aid or you need to pay towards the costs.

We use a trusted open banking provider called TrueLayer to safely access your bank account information.

TrueLayer is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will not:

  • access your data without your permission
  • ask for your online banking username and password
  • share your data with anyone other than the LAA
  • have ongoing access to your bank account information

What you'll need

Your online banking sign-in details.
