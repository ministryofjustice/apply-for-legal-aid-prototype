We need to check your client's income


To help us do this, we need your client’s bank statements from the last 3 months.

Your client can share their statements with us quickly and securely via a third-party called TrueLayer.

What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a trusted third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your client's bank and the LAA.

It allows your client to quickly and securely share their bank statements without having to gather physical bank statements.

They’re regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will:

  • not store your client's bank account details
  • not see your client's online banking sign-in details
  • not share your client's data with anyone other than the LAA
  • not have ongoing access to your client's bank accounts

Your client will need to:

  1. Access 'Apply for legal aid' using a link we'll send them in an email.
  2. Select all the banks they use online banking for.
  3. Allow TrueLayer to collect 3 months of bank statements after we redirect your client to their bank's website or app.

Once they've shared their bank statements, you'll be able to view their transactions and sort them into categories.
