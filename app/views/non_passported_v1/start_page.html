Apply for legal aid

You must be a civil or family legal aid provider to use this service.

Check if you can get legal aid if you need help paying for legal representation.

Use this service to apply for civil legal aid for:

  • public family law
  • domestic violence or abuse

Use the Client and Cost Management System (CCMS) for all other applications.

Before you start

You can only use this service if your client:

  • has a National Insurance number
  • uses online banking
  • is single, does not share an income, or their partner is the opponent in the case
