About the online financial assessment

Your client will be asked to sign in to online banking and give temporary access to their bank accounts.

They'll need to share all their bank accounts, including any in joint names or with no money.

How we use your client's details

We:

  • download 3 months of bank transactions
  • only use information for the legal aid application
  • automatically end the bank connection after one hour
  • do not save online banking details

Give your client access to the financial assessment

When you click 'Submit' we'll send your client a secure link to the online financial assessment.
