About the online financial assessment

Your client will need to:

  • sign in to online banking
  • give us temporary access to all of their bank accounts
  • answer questions about their income, regular costs and assets

How we use your client's details

We:

  • download 3 months of bank transactions
  • only use the information for the legal aid application
  • do not save any sign in details

Give your client access to the financial assessment

When you click 'Send client link' we'll send them a secure link to the online assessment using the email address you provided.
