Sam Doe's financial assessment


Your client needs to do the following:

1. Access 'Apply for legal aid'

We'll send them an email with a link to the service.

2. Sign in to their online banking

They'll be redirected from the service to their bank's website or app.

3. Allow TrueLayer to collect 3 months of bank statements

After this they'll be returned to 'Apply for legal aid'.

4. Tell us about their income and outgoings

We'll ask them to select from a list of income types and allowable expenses.

This will create categories that you’ll need to add your client's bank transactions to when you continue the application.
