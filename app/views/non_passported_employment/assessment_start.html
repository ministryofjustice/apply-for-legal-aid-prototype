Before you continue

What your client has to do

Your client needs to access 'Apply for legal aid' using a link we'll send them in an email.

They'll then be redirected to their bank’s website or app where they can:

  • sign in to their online banking
  • allow TrueLayer to collect 3 months of bank statements on the LAA's behalf

Once they've done this, your client will be returned to 'Apply for legal aid' to answer questions about their income and outgoings.

Merits assessment

You'll need details of your client’s capital, including any savings, investments and other assets.

You'll also need to provide:

  • details of the latest domestic abuse incident
  • information about the opponent
  • a statement of case
  • the chances of a successful outcome
