Share bank statements using online banking


You can share your statements with us through online banking via a third party called TrueLayer.

It's a fast, safe and accurate way to share your statements and means you do not need to gather statements.


What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a trusted third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your bank and the LAA.

TrueLayer needs one-time, read-only access to your bank account information. If you agree, TrueLayer will securely collect 3 months of statements and share them with the LAA.

They're regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will not:

  • store your bank account information
  • see your online banking sign-in details
  • share your data with anyone other than the LAA
  • have ongoing access to your bank accounts

How it works


1. Select your bank

You can do this on the next page.

2. Sign in to online banking

We'll redirect you to your bank's website or app to do this.


3. Share your bank accounts

Your bank will ask you to give TrueLayer access to your bank account information. You must do this for all of your bank accounts.


4. Confirmation

You'll be returned to this service and see a message confirming you've successfully shared your bank statements.

5. What happens next

Your solicitor will use your statements to sort your transactions into categories to help us understand your financial situation.
