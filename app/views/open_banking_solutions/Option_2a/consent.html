We need your client's bank statements from the last 3 months

Your client can quickly and safely share their bank statements with us online through a third-party provider called TrueLayer.

What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a trusted third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your client's bank and the LAA.

With your client's permission, TrueLayer has one-time, read-only access to your client's bank account information, including their bank statements.

TrueLayer shares the bank statements with us so your client does not have to gather physical statements.

TrueLayer is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will not:

  • store your client's bank account information
  • see your client's online banking sign-in details
  • share your client's data with anyone other than the LAA
  • have ongoing access to your client's bank accounts
