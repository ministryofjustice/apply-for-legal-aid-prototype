We need your client's bank statements from the last 3 months

Your client can quickly and securely share their bank statements with us online via a third-party called TrueLayer.

What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a trusted third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your client's bank and the LAA.

It allows your client to share their bank statements without having to gather physical bank statements.

They’re regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will:

  • not store your client's bank account details
  • not see your client's online banking sign-in details
  • not share your client's data with anyone other than the LAA
  • not have ongoing access to your client's bank accounts

How your client can share their bank statements

  1. They access 'Apply for legal aid' using a link we'll send them in an email.
  2. They select the banks they use online banking with.
  3. We redirect your client to their bank's website or app.
  4. They give consent for TrueLayer to securely access their account details.
  5. TrueLayer shares their bank statements with us.

Can your client share their bank statements with us via TrueLayer?

Your client must have an email address and use online banking for all of their current accounts.
You'll need to upload bank statements. Your application may take longer to process as a caseworker will need to check your client's bank statements.
