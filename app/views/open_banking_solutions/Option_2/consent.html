How will you share your client’s bank statements with the LAA?

We need to see your client’s bank statements from the last 3 months as part of their income assessment.

What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a third-party service that acts as a messenger between your client's bank and the LAA.

It allows your client to quickly and securely share their bank statements without having to gather physical bank statements.

They’re regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will not:

  • store your client's bank account details
  • see your client's online banking sign-in details
  • share your client's data with anyone other than the LAA
  • have ongoing access to your client's bank accounts
