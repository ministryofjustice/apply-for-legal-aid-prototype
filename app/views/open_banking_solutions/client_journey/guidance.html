Share your bank statements using online banking


How it works

1. Select your bank on the next page.

2. We’ll then redirect you to your bank’s website or app.

3. Sign in to online banking.

4. Your bank will ask you to give a trusted third party called TrueLayer access to your bank account information. You must do this for all of your bank accounts.

What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your bank and the LAA.

With your consent, they’ll have one-off, read-only access to your account details including:

  • bank statements and transaction details
  • account number and sort code
  • account balances
  • Direct Debits and standing orders

They're regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will not:

  • store your bank account information
  • see your online banking sign-in details
  • share your data with anyone other than the LAA
  • have ongoing access to your bank accounts

5. TrueLayer shares 3 months of your bank statements with us.

6. You’ll be returned to this service to complete your part of the application.
