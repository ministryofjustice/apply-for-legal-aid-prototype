Share your bank statements using online banking


1. Select your bank

You can do this on the next page.

2. Sign in to online banking

We’ll redirect you to your bank’s website or app after you select your bank.

3. Share your bank account information

You’ll be asked to give a trusted third party called TrueLayer one-time,  read-only access to your account details.

What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your bank and the LAA.

With your consent, they’ll have secure, temporary access to your account details including:

  • bank statements and transaction details
  • account number and sort code
  • account balances
  • Direct Debits and standing orders

They're regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will:

  • not store your bank account information
  • not see your online banking sign-in details
  • not share your data with anyone other than the LAA
  • not have ongoing access to your bank accounts

4. TrueLayer shares your bank statements with us

We’ll share these with your solicitor who’ll sort your transactions into categories to help us understand your financial situation.
