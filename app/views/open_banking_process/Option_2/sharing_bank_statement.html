How the process works


1. Select your bank

2. Sign in to online banking

We’ll redirect to your client to your bank’s website or app.

3. Authorise Truelayer to collect 3 months of bank statements

Your bank will ask if you give consent to Truelayer to securely access your account information.

4. TrueLayer fetches bank details and verifies ownership

TrueLayer's name-matching engine compares your name to your name on file at the bank.

5. TrueLayer shares your bank statements with the LAA

You will then be able to continue within the Apply service.
