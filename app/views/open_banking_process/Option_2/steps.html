What your client needs to do


1. Access ‘Apply for legal aid’

We’ll send them an email with a link to the service.

2. Select their bank

We’ll then redirect to your client to their bank’s website or app.

3. Sign in to online banking

4. Authorise TrueLayer to collect 3 months of bank statements

Their bank will ask them to give consent to TrueLayer to securely access their account information.

5. TrueLayer fetches bank details and verifies ownership

TrueLayer’s name-matching engine compares your name to your name on file at the bank.

6. TrueLayer shares your client’s bank statements with the LAA

They’ll then be redirected to ‘Apply for legal aid’ to complete their part of the application.
