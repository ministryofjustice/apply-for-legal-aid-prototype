We need your client's bank statements from the last 3 months

Your client can securely share their bank statements through online banking via a third party called TrueLayer.

It’s a quicker and more accurate way to get a legal aid decision than collecting, scanning and uploading bank statements.


What is TrueLayer?

TrueLayer is a trusted third-party financial service provider that acts as a messenger between your client's bank and the Legal Aid Agency (LAA).

With your client's permission, TrueLayer has one-time, read-only access to their bank account information (including bank statements which TrueLayer shares with us).

TrueLayer is regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) and will not:

  • store your client's bank account information
  • see your client's online banking sign-in details
  • share your client's data with anyone other than the LAA
  • have ongoing access to your client's bank accounts
