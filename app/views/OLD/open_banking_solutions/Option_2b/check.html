How your client can share their bank statements


1. Access 'Apply for legal aid'

We'll send them an email with a link to the service.

2. Select the banks they use online banking with

They'll then be redirected to their bank's website or app.

3. Give consent for TrueLayer to securely access their account details.

TrueLayer will share their bank statements with us.

3. Tell us about their income and outgoings

They'll need to select the categories their income and outgoings fall into.
