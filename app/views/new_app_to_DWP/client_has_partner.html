Does your client have a partner?

A partner is someone your client:

  • is married to
  • is in a civil partnership with
  • normally lives with as a couple, even if they are not living together right now - for example, if one person is in hospital, prison or abroad

Do not include anyone who is separated from your client in a way that is likely to be permanent.
