Does your client recieve any disregarded benefits?

What are disregarded benefits?
These types of benefits are excluded from the financial eligibility calculation.  If your client receives any of these benefits, you’ll need to select the payments from their bank transactions so we’ll know what to exclude from the calculation. These include:
  • Attendance Allowance
  • Armed forces independence payment
  • Carer’s Allowance
  • Council Tax Benefit
  • Disability living allowance
  • Exceptionally Severe Disablement Allowance
  • Housing benefit
  • Personal Independence Payment
  •  Payment on Account of Benefit
    •  
  • Severe Disablement Allowance
  • Social Fund
  •  Social Fund Funeral Payment
    •   
  • War Widows Pension 
  • Widow’s Pension
