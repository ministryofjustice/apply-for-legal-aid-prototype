We need to check if you qualify financially for legal aid.

How we do this

The Legal Aid Agency (LAA) uses a secure third-party service called TrueLayer to connect you to your online banking.

Once you've signed in to online banking, you'll be asked to give TrueLayer one-time access to your bank accounts.

If you agree, TrueLayer will temporarily access your bank account information and share it with the LAA.

We then share your bank statements from the past 3 months with your solicitor to help them check if you qualify financially for legal aid.

What you'll need

Your online banking sign-in details.
