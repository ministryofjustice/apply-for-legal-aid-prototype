Apply for legal aid

Use this service to apply for civil legal aid for:

  • public family law
  • domestic violence or abuse

Use CCMS for all other legal aid applications.

Who can use this service?

You can only use this service if your client:

  • has a National Insurance number
  • uses online banking
  • is single, does not share an income, or their partner is the opponent in the case

You cannot upload documents using this service. Use CCMS if you want to submit evidence to support your case.
