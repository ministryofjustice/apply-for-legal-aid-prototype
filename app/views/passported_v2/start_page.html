Apply for civil legal aid

Civil and family legal aid providers can use this service to apply for civil legal aid.

When not to use this service 

Do not use this service if your client:

  • is under 16 years old
  • self-employed
  • has a partner, unless the case is against the partner

You will need to apply using CCMS (Client and Cost Management System) instead.
